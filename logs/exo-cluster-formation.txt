EXO Cluster Formation Log — Dell + Mini
Date: June 8, 2026

Objective:
Test whether two Linux nodes can automatically form an EXO cluster.

Nodes:
- Dell OptiPlex (Master candidate)
- Mini PC (Worker candidate)

Procedure:
- Started EXO on Dell
- Started EXO on Mini
- Observed discovery and leader election

Observed Output:
Dell elected itself as Master.
Mini discovered Dell and became Worker.

Result:
Successful formation of a 2-node EXO cluster.

Key Finding:
EXO automatically performs peer discovery and leader election without manual configuration.

Importance:
This was the first confirmed multi-node EXO cluster in the project.
